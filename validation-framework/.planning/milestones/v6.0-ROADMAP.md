# Milestone v6.0: Full-Stack Validation Platform

**Status:** SHIPPED 2026-01-26
**Phases:** 17-20
**Total Plans:** 12

## Overview

Transform the validation framework from MVP to production-grade platform with full observability, deep validators, hardened runtime, and multi-project support.

| Decision | Rationale | Outcome |
